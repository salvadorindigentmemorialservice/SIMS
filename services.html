Plan Features & Benefits

Our pre-need plans offer flexibility and peace of mind.

Key Plan Features

  • Transferable: You may transfer the plan to any living person.
  • Assignable: You may assign the plan benefits to any deceased person.
  • Loanable: Loan options available after the plan is fully paid.
  • Withdrawable: Cash value accessible after 10 years of being fully paid (terms apply).
  • Money Back Option: Potential fund return 20 years after being fully paid (terms apply).
  • No Medical Exam: No medical examination required for eligibility.
  • No Age Limits: Open to members of all ages.

Special Accidental Death Benefit

In case of accidental death of the plan holder, the remaining balance is waived, and the chosen memorial package is provided free of charge (terms and conditions apply).
