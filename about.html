Our Mission

For over 21 years, Salvador Indigent Memorial Services has remained family-owned and committed to providing the residents of Sinayawan Valencia City with gentle, compassionate care; continuous grief support; and uniquely personalized memorial services and products at an outstanding value.

Our Vision

We aspire to be the memorial service provider of choice for all families in our community, recognized for our unwavering compassion, integrity, creativity, and commitment to value.

Our Commitment to Care

We genuinely show concern, empathy, and consideration for the needs and well-being of others. We understand the profound feelings that loss creates and approach every interaction with sensitivity. Providing comfort during times of need is the highest calling of our profession.
